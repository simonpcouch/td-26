## bluffbench story

- Was building an agent a couple years ago, want to test its vision
- Show it a common plot but flip the axes--can it tell?
- Tried for days and days to get it to see the plot
- Tried something else--random number of points and colors--it worked
- The eval was born

_Agents enact a performance of progress._ This is a different thing than actually moving your data work forward.
